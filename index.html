Welcome!

I’m Camilo Garrido, a mechanical engineering student at Caltech focused on robotics, product design, and systems engineering. I’m driven by a deep curiosity for how things work—and how thoughtful engineering can make them work better.

I’ve had the privilege of applying this passion across a wide range of projects and industries. Most recently, I served as a Product Design intern at Apple, contributing to the development of next-generation iPhone hardware through mechanical system integration, CAD refinement, prototyping, and failure analysis. I’ve also worked in thermal systems at Trane Technologies, taught engineering fundamentals as a Caltech TA, and built robots and mechanical systems ranging from transmissions to tethered crater-descending vehicles.

Whether I’m designing hardware, modeling dynamic systems, machining parts, or integrating electronics and software, I love the challenge of bringing complex ideas into reality. This site is a curated space to share the engineering work, robotics projects, and long-term builds that have shaped my journey so far.

Thanks for visiting—and I hope you enjoy exploring my work.
